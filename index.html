Improving African Healthcare

Our Story

Medica Overseas has been dedicated to improving healthcare in Africa since 2008. With a team of experienced professionals and strong partnerships across various fields and countries, we specialize in health tourism, medical event management, and medical education. Our main office is located in Cairo, Egypt, with additional offices in Mansoura, Egypt, and Kano, Nigeria. Our mission is to bridge gaps in healthcare access and enhance the quality of medical services across the continent.

Our Mission

At Medica Overseas, our mission is to transform healthcare in Africa by providing accessible, high-quality medical services through health tourism, innovative medical event management, and comprehensive medical education. We are dedicated to fostering strong partnerships and leveraging expertise to bridge gaps in healthcare access and enhance the overall well-being of communities across the continent.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in the evolution of healthcare in Africa, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and collaboration. We aspire to create a future where every individual has access to world-class medical services, contributing to healthier communities and sustainable development across the continent.
